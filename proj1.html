CSE 555 (Computational Photography) Project 1

High-Dynamic Range Imaging and Tone Mapping

Mark Heimann & Jing Dao Chen 2/9/2015

Overview

The aim of the project is to construct a high dynamic range radiance map from a series of photographs taken at different exposures. The technique used is an implementation of the paper Recovering High Dynamic Range Radiance Maps from Photographs. Paul E. Debevec and Jitendra Malik. SIGGRAPH 97. Here we also explore methods to address problems in the original technique including calculation of the ğ›Œ parameter and sampling of pixels.
